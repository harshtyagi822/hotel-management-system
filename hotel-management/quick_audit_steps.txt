UI audit quick steps:
1) Open hotel-management/index.html in browser.
2) Confirm topbar links (Contact/Admin) are visible.
3) Resize to 320px width: ensure navbar hamburger works and links are visible.
4) Ensure Book Now button is clickable (click it).
5) Scroll to counters: verify numbers are visible and animate.
6) Open gallery: click tiles and verify lightbox layering.
7) Open booking: verify summary visible and no overlay covers submit.
8) Open dashboard: ensure charts render and not hidden.
